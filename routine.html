Personal Bonsai Daily Care Routine

This routine was developed by almost killing my first Bonsai (Groot) due to over watering and pruning.

  1. Touch the soil and in some cases pinch the dirt to see if it is moist.
  2. Visually inspect the Bonsai and make mental notes of how it looks that day.
  3. Water every other day depending on the soil moisture, which depends on the temperature inside the apartment. I only use unfiltered water. Some times when it rains I have a few glass jars outside that I use to collect water to use.
  4. Rotate position of pot every day.
  5. Clean up any fallen leaves and make note of such event.
  6. Remove with scissors any dead branches a few times a year, usually in the winter season.
